Keyboard

/ˈkiːbɔːd/

noun

Meaning

  • (etc.) A set of keys used to operate a typewriter, computer etc.
  • A component of many instruments including the piano, organ, and harpsichord
  • A device with keys of a musical keyboard, used to control electronic sound-producing devices which may be build into or seperate from the keyboard device.
